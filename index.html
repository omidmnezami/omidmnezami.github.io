Postdoctoral Research Fellow

Macquarie University



I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow working with Mark Dras in the Department of Computing, Faculty of Science and Engineering, Macquarie University, Sydney, NSW, Australia. My main research interests are in the areas of Deep Neural Networks, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Robotics and Health Informatics.

I received my PhD in Computer Science from Macquarie University where I was advised by Mark Dras and Len Hamey. I was fortunate to work with Stephen Wan and Cecile Paris from CSIRO's Data61 as well.

I like playing squash and biking around when I have free time.
