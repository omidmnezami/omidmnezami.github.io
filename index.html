Biography


I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science. My main research interest centres on Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and Robotics.

My current focus is on automatic image captioning where vision and language techniques are combined to generate captions for describing visual content. I also work on object detection models aiming to detect bounding boxes containing objects in addition to classifying them in an image. For my PhD thesis, I am working under the supervision of Mark Dras and Len Hamey at Macquarie University. I also work under the supervision of Stephen Wan and Cecile Paris at Data61, CSIRO.

Previously, I worked for several years as a lecturer at Azad University. Prior to working at Azad University, I was a research scientist at Mechatronics Research Laboratory, Iran.
